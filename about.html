About

The Dining Board has been serving up gormet charcuterie boards since 2017. The owner started this business as an accident, but truly is excited with the outcome. The Dining Boards serves a variety of different boards that range from our Award Winning cheese and meat arrangement, to dessert, and our personal favorite...the Taco Board.
